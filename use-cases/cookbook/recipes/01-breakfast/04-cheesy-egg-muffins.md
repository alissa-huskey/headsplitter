Cheesy Egg Muffins
==================

Ingredients
-----------

* 12 eggs
* 2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
* 1 cup parmesan cheese, shredded
* 1/4 tsp oregano
* 1/4 tsp basil
* Salt, to taste
* Pepper, to taste

### Instructions

1. Line a muffin pan.
2. Combine all ingredients and distribute the batter evenly.
3. Bake at 350℉ for 20-­25 minutes.
