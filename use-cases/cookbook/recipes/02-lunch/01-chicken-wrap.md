Chicken Wrap
============

An easy, healthy grab-and-go meal.

Ingredients
-----------

* 2 cooked chicken breasts, cubed
* lettuce
* caesar dressing
* baby tomatoes, halved
* grated armesan cheese
* croutons
* 4 10" tortillas

Instructions
------------

1. Toss lettuce and dressing.
3. Assemble ingredients.
